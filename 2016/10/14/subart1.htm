Bernie Sanders Endorses Orville Redenbacher's Gourmet Popping Corn™ as Best Presidential Debate Snack

By THE EDITORIAL BOARD

October 14th, 2016

VERMONT - In the wake of the most recent presidential debate, one important political figure has been ignored: Vermont Senator and former Democratic presidential candidate Bernie Sanders. The Clinton-Trump drama, polarized America, and the unexpected Ken Bone debut has overshadowed the celebrated social democrat. Fortunately, Lawrenceville Out Loud was able to secure an exclusive interview with him during the screening of the debate.

When asked about his predictions for the debate, Sanders said, “I’m sure Donald is going to drop some, what would Hillary try to call it, oh yes, ‘fire roasts.’ That’ll be entertaining. Oh, just a minute.” Sanders paused the interview to grab his Orville Redenbacher's Gourmet Popping Corn™ that had been cooking in the microwave. During Trump’s attack on the DNC’s rigging of the primaries in Hillary’s favor, a muffled grunt was heard from Sanders as he poured salt over his popcorn. Sanders enjoys his popcorn and politics salty.

After the debate concluded, Sanders offered his opinion on the presidential situation. Sadly turning towards the television, he remarked “the only real loser of this race is going to be America… oh, and [me] because, you know, I lost the candidacy for the Democratic presidential nominee. Nothing I could've done about that. I guess that was just one of those things that didn’t click between us.”

At press time, the senator was crying into a pint of ice cream while scrolling through the Facebook page of the Democratic party while unliking their past posts.
